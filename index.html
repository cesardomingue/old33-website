Our Story

A Local Spot Worth the Drive

Old 33 is a cozy brick pub sitting right on Rockingham Street in the heart of Elkton, Virginia — the kind of place where you know the staff by name and the food is made the way it should be.

We use 100% grass-fed beef, keep local craft beers on tap, and take pride in every plate that leaves the kitchen. Whether you're coming off a Shenandoah hike or just want a great burger with a cold beer, you're welcome here.
