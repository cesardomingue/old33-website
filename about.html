Our Story

About Old 33

Who We Are

More Than Just a Burger Joint

Nestled on West Rockingham Street in the charming small town of Elkton, Virginia, Old 33 Beer & Burger Grill is a beloved local landmark where community gathers and great food is always on the menu.

We're named after Route 33, the road that runs through the Shenandoah Valley and brings travelers, hikers, and locals right to our door. Our cozy brick building features arched doorways, dark wood tables, a welcoming bar, and sports TVs that set the perfect laid-back pub atmosphere.

What sets us apart? We use 100% grass-fed beef in every single burger, because quality ingredients make all the difference. Pair that with local beers on tap, generous portions, and a staff that treats you like family.
